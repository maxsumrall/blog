About

I'm a software engineer at ABN AMRO in Amsterdam, The Netherlands. I help build software used anytime you pay with a debit card.

I have a Masters of Computer Science from the Technical University of Eindhoven and a Bachelors of Computer Science from George Mason University.

Tech and programming is more than just a job. I'm deeply motivated and a fast learner. I'm trying to be around the best people and learn why and how things work.
